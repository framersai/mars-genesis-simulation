What Is This?

Mars Genesis is a live multi-agent simulation. Two AI commanders lead the same colony of 100 colonists through crises spanning 50 years on Mars. Their HEXACO personality profiles drive different decisions, different tool inventions, and different civilizational outcomes. The simulation is deterministic: same seed, same starting colony. Only leadership differs.

How It Works

  • Deterministic kernel handles colonist generation, aging, births, deaths, career progression, and resource accounting. Seeded RNG (Mulberry32) ensures reproducibility.
  • Department agents (Medical, Engineering, Agriculture, Psychology, Governance) analyze each crisis using real Mars science with DOI-linked citations.
  • Tool forging lets department agents invent computational tools at runtime (radiation models, food projectors, structural analyzers). An LLM judge reviews each tool for safety and correctness before approval.
  • Personality drift evolves promoted colonists' HEXACO traits over time via leader pull, role pull, and outcome feedback.
  • Commanders decide which policies to enact. Their personality shapes risk tolerance, expansion pace, and resource allocation.

What Do The Numbers Mean?

  • Pop = alive colonists. Starts at 100. Changes from births, deaths, and immigration decisions.
  • Morale = colony-wide wellbeing (0-100%). Affected by food reserves, overcrowding, and policy decisions.
  • Food = months of food reserves remaining.
  • Deaths = cumulative colonist deaths from aging, radiation, and crisis outcomes.
  • Tools Forged = computational tools invented by department agents during the simulation.
  • HEXACO bars = personality traits: Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, Emotionality, Honesty-Humility.

What Is Personality Drift?

Promoted colonists' HEXACO traits change over time through three forces: leader pull (traits converge toward the commander), role pull (department roles activate specific traits), and outcome pull (successful risks boost openness, failed risks boost conscientiousness). Grounded in real personality psychology research.
